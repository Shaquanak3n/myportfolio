Welcome to Lulu's High Tea!




Lulu's High Tea offers an elevated tea experience inspired by elegant afternoon tea traditions with a modern Jamaican twist. Rooted in the heart of Jamaica, our business provides premium tea blends, savory treats, and sweet indulgences in a serene setting. Whether for casual meet-ups, celebrations, or moments of self-care, Lulu's High Tea promises a calming, uplifting, and memorable experience.



Our Services


  1. Afternoon tea services five days a week

  2. Customized high tea menus

  3. Tea and wine tasting sessions for each guest
